Components of a computer

The following is a list of major components of a computer and what they do:

Case
Holds all the components
Motherboard
Everything is plugged into this component, the "main hub"
RAM
Holds all the components
RAM
Random Access Memory(RAM) enables your computer to do short term tasks, like browser tabs.
CPU
The brain of the computer, this is where the magic happens.
GPU
Your graphics card is what makes things look pretty!
SSD
Holds data, faster than a traditonal harddrive.
HDD
Holds data, usually bigger in capcity than SSDs, tho slower
Cooling
Fans, radiators, watercooling are all systems that help keep your system running cool!
